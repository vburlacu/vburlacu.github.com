  • About the Founder
    Sean Mehner, CISA, CISSP
    Sean has spent a majority of his career focused on providing first class security reviews of Internet-facing technologies. He has a wealth of experience having worked with organizations of all sizes in industries ranging from e-Commerce to Financial Services. Having worked with clients such as Avon Products, DHL, and Priceline.com, Sean has implemented and reviewed a wide variety of complex IT systems. He has also helped to build the security architectures of small, local organizations looking to secure their IT operations. Sean is a Certified Information Systems Security Professional(CISSP) and Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA).
